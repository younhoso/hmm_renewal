Meeting the
first Voyage
of the
Mother
Vessel HMM
Promise

Cintia Cabral Ribeiro / HMM Brazil

After months of countdown and anticipation, on Monday, August 13th, we arrived in the Multiseas agent office excited to meet the biggest HMM vessel in Brazil, HMM Promise 001, in her maiden voyage.

Previously, our Liner Manager, Mr. Pedro Oliveira, had offered his time to provide us some lessons from his experiences in shipping, where we learned about ship particulars such as deadweight, bow thruster, and LBP (length between perpendiculars), in addition to vessel procedures to birth in Brazil ports.

On August 14th, we went together from São Paulo to Santos city to meet the HMM Promise at DP World Terminal. When we arrived, we were received by Mr. Marcelo Procopio, Mr. Paulo Cesar Turini and Mr. Thiago Martins from the Commercial Department, and we all went together to see the most beautiful lady HMM Promise which was already berthed.

Multiseas Commercial Team and DP World Team

According to the Brazilian newspaper, A Tribuna, besides its 329m LOA, the vessel is 48m wide, with 16.02m of maximum draft, and weighs almost 134,419 tons. Built in May 2018, the ship sails under the Marshall Islands flag. At its biggest capacity, the HMM Promise can load 11.000 TEUs.

On board, we met Master Mr. Ko Beongchan, Chief Officer Mr. Oh Kyoung Je, Second Officer Mr. Shin Seonho and Chief Engineer Mr. Bag Jong Mun, who welcomed us with kind attention and explained detailed information about the vessel’s navigation and engine devices.

Multiseas and DP World team together with Chief Engineer Mr. Bag Jong Mun, Second Officer Mr. Shin Seonho and their team at the engineering room

From left to right: Second Officer Mr. Shin Seonho, Chief Officer Mr. Oh Kyoung Je and Captain Mr. Ko Beongchan, with Multiseas and DP World Team on board

From left to right: Mr. Song – HMM Owns representative; Mr. Marcelo Procópio - Commercial Manager from DP World Santos, and Captain Mr. Ko Beongchan; Captain Ko received a special plaque from DP World terminal

HMM Promise is a brand new container ship, fully loaded with the most modern systems. Her scrubber was installed to ensure compliance with new environmental regulations that shall come in force in 2020.This system was developed to reduce sulphur and CO² emissions, and her engine ensures optimal economic speed and performance, and is expected to save fuel. We also learned about bunker consumption variation towards eco, cruze and maximum steering.

Multiseas Agency has been honored to act as HMM’s General Agents in Brazil for over 12 years. Thank you HMM for the greatest experience and opportunity. Let’s continue our ongoing partnership!

Editor Multiseas Team, from left to right: Victor Sakurata, Caroline Machioni, Cintia Ribeiro and Isabella Frances
