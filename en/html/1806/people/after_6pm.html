After 6PM

HMM Terminal
Lego Mockup

Fabio Bianchi / HMM Italy

Hello from Italy!

My name is Fabio Bianchi, and I’ve been with the HMM family since 2006. I first started my career with the company in the import department, and for the past six years, I’ve been a dedicated member of the booking office and customer service department in Genoa, which is the headquarter of HMM Italy.

Last December, our office was relocated from the western part of Genoa to the beautiful city center. As part of the move, everything was new: furniture, computers, desks, and the office building. However, even though the relocation was amazing, I realized that there was still one thing missing.

As I reflected upon HMM’s new service and cooperation with 2M, an idea popped into my mind: why were Lego toys only be used for the Maersk logo? Why not for HMM too? Over the past winter, I studied many ways to elaborate upon my idea, and looked into many sites including E-bay and others to bring my idea to fruition.

Day by day, I received various items by post. Every evening I worked for about two hours to complete the HMM terminal mockup. Gradually, I added the railways, train, three cranes, reach stacker, trucks, and finally containers (dry, reefer and flat rack containers).

Every day I made some modifications to make the terminal more realistic. For example, it took lots of time to design the flat rack container.

Now it was time to design and print out stickers with the HMM logo and container numbers to complete the project.

(Note: I really hope to receive the OOG acceptance by our R.O.C. office for lashing/security, and seaworthy safety approval)

Now it was time to design and print out stickers with the HMM logo and container numbers to complete the project.

As the last step, I fixed all pieces on top of a wooden plate and covered it using a glass box for protection.

The whole project weight is over 25kg, and thanks to my colleague Giulio, at the end of the March I finally delivered this special present to our new HMM office. Surprisingly, our Managing Director Mr. Hyunho Seok, and our HR manager Mr. Reza Atri, decided to place the mockup at the entrance of the office for all to see.

I would like to dedicate this special present to the HMM family and in appreciation for all my wonderful colleagues as well as our managing directors, Mr. D.H. Oh, Mr. K.I. Jung, Mr. eSop Kim and Mr. Hyunho Seok.
