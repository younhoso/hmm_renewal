What's NEW

What's NEW

HMM IHQ head office based out of Mumbai, India recently relocated to brand new premises.

To give the honours, HMM CEO & President Mr. C.K. Yoo, on 4 April 2018, personally walked the red carpet, cut the ribbon and officially opened the premises for business.

The move also commemorated 23 years of HMM operations in India, which has only been possible with each one of our colleague’s hard work and dedication.

The entire Mumbai staff of 100 plus colleagues, full of excitement and enthusiasm, and holding India & HMM flags, welcomed Mr. C.K Yoo and cheered the ribbon cutting ceremony.

This was followed by a lamp lighting, an ancient Indian tradition of lighting oil lamps for auspiciousness, prosperity and abundance. Mr. D.H. Oh, Managing Director HMM India Pvt. Ltd., leading from the front walked into the new office and individually welcomed all colleagues with gratitude and humility.

Then, there was a cake cutting, followed by champagne opening, with a loud toast to the bright future of not only HMM India, but HMM as a whole.

The move to the new premises coincides with India being elevated from a subsidiary to headquarter status and globally recognized as a priority emerging market.
Specifically, India is projected to grow at 7.4% in 2018, while the global growth forecast stands at 3.9%.

Additionally, India is the world's fastest growing economy with growth accelerating to 7.8% in 2019-2020.
Economic reforms such as demonetization and GST are finally stabilizing, and with the strong focus on Make in India, and Sagarmala, we are confident that India has arrived.

With our AEX service, a direct Asia – North Europe Product calling Colombo effective April 2018, we are increasing our footprint by adding more to our already diverse portfolio.

Also with the pricing team (TMT) now centralized in IHQ itself, we are able to drive more businesses at a much faster pace and customers are appreciating the same.

Many customers sent in their congratulations and good wishes in the form of bouquets and cards to mark this occasion and bless HMM India with all the success.

This is just a beginning; HMM India has a long way to go.

All the very best “Team IHQ!”

HMM President & CEO, Mr. C.K. Yoo also visited GDC India on April 6. Let’s see the pictures of his first visit at GDC India. (Photos provided by Mimoh Shewale / GDC Mumbai)
