HMM Honored At Intermodal Fair By Sepetiba Tecon

Tatiane Oliveira / HMM Brasil

< Source : http://www.intermodal.com.br/pt/ >

Once per year, São Paulo city in Brazil hosts one of the most important events for the logistics, cargo transport and foreign trade industries of Latin America, The Intermodal Fair.

The fair features three days of business opportunities and speeches with various meetings of representatives from both the public and private sectors to discuss various issues related to the market. Every year at Intermodal, visitors can also find a complete grid of lectures and forums to learn the latest market trends and insights.

In 2018, the fair featured more than 400 exhibitors, including Sepetiba Tecon. Located in Rio de Janeiro, the company is considered one of Brazil‘s main container terminals, offering a modern infrastructure and strategic location to importers and exporters from the most important destinations and origins.

Among its operations, Sepetiba operates at Itaguaí port, and due to its location and infrastructure, HMM partners with Sepetiba in moving various cargo in out of the port. As part of the partnership, HMM also works closely with members of Sepetiba’s commercial team, including Mr. Bruno Januzzi, Commercial Manager, and Bruna Nascimento, Sales Representative. Always gentle and ready to help, they are instrumental to the smooth communication so important to the success of the partnership.

As a result of the good relation between our two companies, this year, HMM was honored by Sepetiba which prepared a presentation of our lady Hyundai Loyalty to display on the main screen of their stand. Everyone that was at the fair could see our vessel and learn that the HMM Hyundai Loyalty is the longest ship to ever operate in Brazilian waters!

Thanks to this vessel, it was possible for Sepetiba to receive long vessels and to start talking to port authorities asking them to increase the draft of the port. In order to try to give visitors a pleasant experience at the fair, Sepetiba Tecon also made a 3D presentation where it was possible to watch M/V Hyundai Loyalty arriving, berthing and operating at port.

We would like to sincerely thank Sepeteiba Tecon for providing us this memorable opportunity. Thank you from HMM!
