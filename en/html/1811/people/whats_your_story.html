HMM Opens Its Own Booth at the Asia Fruit Logistica 2018 in Hong Kong

From September 5 to 7, 2018, HMM opened its own booth in the Asia Fruit Logistica in Hong Kong, becoming, for the first time, an official organizer. In this article, Assistant Manager Hyeongmin Kang of the Reefer Business Management Team, introduces his experience preparing for and hosting this event.

01Entrance to Hong Kong Asia World-Expo Exhibition Hall where the event was held

02Entrance to Asia Fruit Logistica 2018

03, 04HMM booth view

The “Hong Kong Asia Fruit Logistica 2018" is the largest exhibition in Asia related to the fresh agricultural produce industry. It has been held in Hong Kong in the first week of September every year since 2007. As of 2017, it has hosted more than 25,000 visitors from about 100 countries around the world and 1,000 organizers from about 60 countries.

This event, along with "Fruit Logistica" held in Berlin every February, and "China Fruit Logistica" held in Shanghai, China every May, showcases the latest trends in the world fresh agricultural products industry. At the same time, it has become the world's largest event that directly connects various sellers, buyers, logistics companies and related industries from all over the world.

This event, which is held annually, features world-renowned fruit companies, such as Dole, Del Monte and Zespri, as well as major fresh produce companies from each participating country. Also, global shipping companies as well as major forwarders, such as 'Kuehne & Nagel', 'Panalpina' and 'Schenker' participate every year. Through these events, they are engaged in various promotional and sales activities, such as promoting their products and services, by meeting with people related to the fresh agricultural products industry.

05, 06, 07, 08Other shipping company’s booth view (MSC, Hapag-loyd, CMA-CGM/APL, ONE)

HMM has participated in these global events as a visitor in the past. However, this year, based on its mid- to long-term RF sales activation scheme and keynote, HMM decided to register as an organizer and open a formal booth, formally submitting an application form in June.

A total of nine shipping companies (HMM, MSC / APL, CMA, Hapag, MSK/Hamburg SUD, COSCO, OOCL, ONE and Wanhai) participated in this event, carrying out their service promotions and sales activities. We also worked hard to keep abreast with other shipping companies by inviting twenty of our sales people from all over the world.

09Event opening group photo

10, 11Main booth view (Korean pavilion, Panalpina)

Indeed, many people put in a lot of work to successfully hold this kind of event. The preparatory process started in earnest after receiving the application form and approval from the event organizer last June.

After getting our application approved, we received bids from various companies to make booths, which were to be the center of our event. We also planned to promote sales activities by producing various promotional brochures, souvenirs, and preparing interviews with related media.

In addition, we selected people from HMM's global network of salesmen to participate in the event to promote our services and conduct sales activities. We also planned our sales and promotional activities as carefully as possible by selecting target shippers in advance, sending invitations and holding meetings with them.

After taking these steps over the past several months to prepare for this event, we finally opened a booth at the event and participated from September 5 to 7. As a result, we were able to build a wide variety of networks with stakeholders throughout the industry (including importers/exporters, other shipping companies, terminal operators, government officials) as well as forwarders and shippers. In particular, HMM’s regional sales representatives intensively promoted our services, by holding dozens of meetings with their respective shippers and vendors from the first day of opening to third day of closing.

As a result of such efforts, we were able to successfully achieve our immediate goal of expanding the scope of our freezing business as well as strengthening our brand image.

With the successful conclusion of the event, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to those who carefully prepared for this event from the application period straight through to the event’s conclusion: Mr. Oh, Seung-woo, Director General of South China Division; Mr. Oh Chang-Kyung, Director; and Shin Suh-ji, Manager of HMM Hong Kong; and Momoko Chan, Sam Hui and many of my colleagues around the world.
