2018 HMM

tion Dinner
Event

Johnson Zhang / HMM Shanghai

In Chinese culture, Autumn is the traditional season for harvest, and the so-called “Golden Season.” In this regard, it is also the perfect time to express gratitude to family, friends and all those who have supported us.

Accordingly, on the evening of October 26, 2018, HMM Shanghai successfully held our 2018 Customer Appreciation Dinner Event at the Shanghai Andaz Hotel, which is one of the city’s best boutique hotels located in the fashionable landmark area of Xin Tian Di.

We invited our valuable customers and partners, and were also very glad to have attendants from HMM HQs top management including, Head of Container Business and Executive Vice President, Mr. SH Kim, and Team Leader of TP Trade, Mr. Peter (TM) Kim. All CHQ and HSQ key personnel also attended this grand ceremony.

A small cocktail party was held during the reception period, and HQ top management Mr. SH Kim, CHQ president Mr. DH Lee, and HSQ Managing Director Mr. YS Choi warmly welcomed all the guests. Following the cocktail reception, everyone gathered together to take a group photo.

To open the event, Mr. SH Kim gave an opening speech, sharing the latest HMM updates to all the guests. There was further strong impression given to all guests on HMM’s grand plan of building 20 new mega ships, and target to increase total capacity to 1 million TEU. At the same time, we played a PR video in Chinese, which all guests enjoyed. We also invited Mr. Time Yang, President of Dewell Group, to give a speech on behalf of all guests. Afterwards, Mr. DH Lee gave a toast in Chinese, and wished all the best for everyone.

When the dinner started, the atmosphere was lively, and everyone enjoyed the delicious foods and dishes over great conversations. The entire dinner last for more than two hours, and the night was full of good memories that all of us will cherish.

As the first event we have held in Shanghai in the last eight years and as we promote our grand plan for 2020-201, HMM’s image in the Shanghai shipping market continues to be further strengthened. We do look forward to holding similar events on a regular basis, which will make HMM more influential as one of the leading carriers.
