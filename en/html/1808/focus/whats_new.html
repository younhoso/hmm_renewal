HMM Viet Nam Staff Outing June 2018- We Are A Team

Nguyen Thuy Thanh Tam / HMM Vietnam

As a reward for the hard-work of all employees, Staff Outing is annually held for the local offices in Viet Nam. This year, it was a great chance for all employees from four offices in the North, Central and South of the country gathering together to visit one of the most beautiful beaches in the South Central Coast of Viet Nam, Cam Ranh Bay -Nha Trang beach.

Cam Ranh city, belonging to Khanh Hoa province, is well-known for its four beautiful ‘Binh’ islands: Binh Ba, Binh Tien, Binh Lap, and Binh Hung.

We chose Binh Hung to spend the 1st day of our trip. Binh Hung is located in Cam Ranh bay with pristine and attractive beauty. The water is clean and clear like crystal so we decided to take a tour seeing creatures diving down under the water. We also contacted with locals for transportation to Bai Chuoi in order to observe the coral. This island is also known under the name Lobster island because it is very famous for big and tasty Lobsters. Therefore, we didn’t hesitate to enjoy a lobster lunch with variety seafood as well.

After coming back to mainland from Binh Hung island by speedboat, we moved to Bai Dai Beach and checked in at the Riviera Beach resort - a lavish 5-start beachfront resort. We had time to take a short rest and prepare for Gala Dinner as well.

Gala Dinner kicked off at 7:00pm under the theme ‘WE ARE A TEAM’. Beginning the party was a very cute and significant video that introduced all employees to one another. The video clip deeply impressed everyone.

After that, Mr. Ju Kyeong Ho, Managing Director of HMM Viet Nam, continued with a speech expressing his happiness for the very special and meaningful occasion. He also addressed his high appreciation to all HMM Viet Nam Staff for the dedication and set up the plan for the growth of HMM in Viet Nam in the coming years.

Mr. Ju’s speech ended with a champagne toasting and cheering. Everyone started to enjoy buffet party with cake, fruit and beer under the vibrant light of music and dancing. The fascinating performances along with some funny games made us let our hair down to join, and all of these things made the party much more special. After the awards announcement for some best performances, the party ended at 10:00pm to the regret of all.

However, instead of watching the World Cup football match between Korean and Mexico or returning to our rooms to sleep, as recommendation from Mr. Ju, we went to the outside stage on the beach making a circle. With a guitarist and many singers, we talked, drank and sang together. In that peaceful moment, I myself suddenly had the feeling of friendship of family not the feeling between boss and employee. Time just flew by from song to song, and we went to our rooms at the midnight.

The next morning, after getting a good night’s sleep, we enjoyed the 5-star resort scenery. With the white salt, sunlight, coconut palms and pristine beach water, it was such a good place to get some rustic photographs. The management said goodbye to us at 10:00am to organize their golf match.

Finally, we checked out from the resort at 11:00am and moved to Nha Trang view restaurant for lunch. After that, we went to the 100 eggs resort to take a mud-bath. The 1st experience of taking a mud-bath has was unforgettable for me.

Time flew so fast. After having dinner together, it was time to say goodbye. We all went back to work with unforgettable memories that remained. Everyone had done great jobs - we all had a great time together.

Returning from the trip, I, a new comer in the probationary period, with the first chance to join a staff outing, realized that HMM is really a second family for all employees in which everyone can work, cooperate and play together. You may need to make your own decisions, but you can not do everything by yourself. And, it is your colleagues who you cooperate and work hard with all year round that help you attain your objectives.

As the saying goes: “If you want to go fast, go alone. But if you want to go far, go together”

“HMM - WE ARE A TEAM


One for all, and all for one”
