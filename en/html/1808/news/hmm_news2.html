HMM and Oracle Sign MOU for
Implementation of ‘Industry 4.0’

On July 12, HMM signed an MOU with Oracle for strategic cooperation to enhance competitiveness in IT based on the 'Industry 4.0(1) trend.

Oracle is expanding their cooperation scope in the IT area with HMM as the developer of the Cloud-based next generation IT system for HMM.

For the signing ceremony hosted at headquarters in Seoul, 30 participants joined, including HMM CEO, C.K. Yoo; SVP for Oracle Asian Region, Roger Li; and President of Oracle Korea, Hyeong-rae Kim.

HMM aims to optimize its applications of operation process innovation and a next generation system Cloud which are targeted for adoption in 2020, through the expansion of cooperation with Oracle. Additionally, various other benefits are expected from the more convenient and stable IT service environment.

As part of its approach, HMM specifically will: 1) establish a Cloud-based operation environment, 2) participate in mutual cooperation between the marine transportation and IT industries for the joint business of Industry 4.0; 3) cooperate in new technology services through the Joint Lab; 4) fortify competitiveness through global brand value improvement; 5) cooperate for the interchange through the Steering Committee between both companies.

Both parties will make their best efforts in particular to improve the competitiveness of HMM by establishing a Joint lab for joint research and to actively implement their cooperation on the adoption of the principal technologies related to the 4th industrial revolution such as Block chain, the Internet of Things, AI, and so forth, which HMM is currently implementing.

As result, HMM expects to improve security, operation processing efficiency and innovative capabilities through the establishment of the Cloud-based operation environment, and Oracle Korea is anticipated to develop capability for a Cloud service which is customized for the marine transportation industry.

An HMM official said, "Through this partnership with Oracle, an excellent IT service provision basis has been prepared which is very different from other global shipping companies. Furthermore, this will be a good basis to take the lead in competition for Industry 4.0 in the marine transportation industry."

A Oracle Korea official explained, "This is a case in which the latest Cloud system will be successfully adopted in the marine transportation industry which is required to process a large amount of distribution information in an accurate and timely manner with the cooperation of various related parties such as the center. Also, the utilization of the Cloud system will serve as an essential approach which will enable the improvement of user convenience and the innovation of actual value."

HMM has focused on the establishment of the Cloud-based next generation system since last year, and officially announced in May that the company designated Oracle as their official partner.

(1) Industry 4.0 (Industry 4.0)

Industry 4.0 is known as the '4th industrial revolution' or the '4th generation industrial production system' and the industrial policy, which optimizes the overall production process by establishing the total automatization of the manufacturing industry where the information interchange between the production equipment and products is enabled through the Internet of Things. Thus, it is a system that jumpstarts the evolution to smart factory by combining the IT system with the traditional business such as the manufacturing industry. This also includes the machineries, industrial equipment and parts of the factory mutual interchange information and data through the information communication technology (ICT), and as artificial intelligence is applied to every machine, all the operation processes can be controlled properly and efficiently
