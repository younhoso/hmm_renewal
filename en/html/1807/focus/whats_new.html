2018 HMM Xiamen & Fuzhou CRM Dinner

Leon Zhang / HMM Xiamen

On June 8, we organized the 2018 HMM Xiamen & Fuzhou CRM dinner in Xiamen city. Through this event, we successfully introduced both our Xiamen direct call new product promotion and Xiamen new organization to all of our guests.

This activity was greatly supported by HMM SEL HQ and the SCHQ Trade Management Team. On behalf of SEL HQ, Mr. SH Kim, Executive Vice President, and Mr. GS Kim, Senior Vice President, visited Xiamen to present at this occasion.

Mr. SW Oh, Managing Director of S. China & HK, led the group of consisting of the SCHQ TMT and SCHQ regional management teams. In addition, Ms. Suji Shin, General Manager of SCHQ TMT, Mr. HB Song, General Manager of SCHQ operation, and Mr. Chris Oh, General Manager of the Hong Kong office, all were fully engaged in the preparation, together with the Xiamen & Fuzhou Office Managers and Sales Teams, to make sure every detail was in good order.

In total, over 130 distinguished guests were included on the invitation list, which included not only external customers, but also the President of Xiamen Port Holding Group, top management of Xiamen Container Terminal Group, and other key persons from different shipping agencies, depots, and feeder suppliers.

As the event kicked off, Mr. SH Kim delivered a welcome speech extending his gratitude to all guests, and also emphasized positive anticipation for the upcoming substantial growth of HMM Fujian (Xiamen & Fuzhou) based on Fujian’s market potential and the HMM local agency’s competencies. Next to share on HMM Fujian’s ambitions, Mr. Leon Zhang, General Manager of Fujian province, presented on HMM’s core values, HMM innovation 2020, and HMM missions. He also shared regarding the XMN new direct service introduction (PS5 & EU5), XMN all trade service overview and HMM Fujian leadership chart. Throughout the speeches, the message was clear: HMM Fujian is ready to take on more responsibility and new challenges in collaboration with SEL HQ and SCHQ to enlarge HMM’s market share in Xiamen and deliver premium customer satisfaction.

The dinner was wonderful, extending for more than two hours, and the dining hall was full with happy laughter. There were smiling faces, handshakes, and happy cheers all around. HMM management led a toast with HMM employees yelling, “Fujian! Fujian! Fujian!”, followed by our guests’ resounding response in chorus, “HMM! HMM! HMM!”.

As the saying goes, never say goodbye in Xiamen.
Let’s keep our fingers crossed for a brilliant tomorrow together as “We Carry the Future!”.
