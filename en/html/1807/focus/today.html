HMM Today

July, a Look at RWG Terminal

Editor

This month, Compass shares photos of the Rotterdam World Gateway (RWG) terminal with readers. HMM holds a 20% stake in RWG terminal, also serving as a member of the terminal’s board of directors.

At the RWG terminal, all processes are highly automated and all cranes and vehicles on the terminal run on electricity 24 hours a day and 7 days a week. The recently launched AEX service also calls this terminal and you can find the vessel, Hyundai Forward, berthed here as well.

Let’s take a look!
