HMM Launches China-Australia Express Service

Seoul, Korea, June 14, 2018, HMM announced that it is launching a new premium China-Australia express service, under the name A1X (Australia No.1 Express) with global carriers, Evergreen and APL.

From August 17, the A1X service will cover the ports of ‘Ningbo-Shanghai-Yantian-Sydney-Melbourne-Brisbane’ starting its first service at Ningbo on the vessel, Hyundai Supreme.

Previously, HMM provided the service through a slot purchase from Maersk, MSC, ONE, and Hamburg Süd.

For the new service, a total of five Panamax (4,600TEU) vessels will be deployed. HMM will deploy two vessels, and Evergreen and APL, will deploy three vessels on the new premium express service.

An HMM official commented, “We are pleased to introduce the premium A1X service to our valued customers. HMM will provide stable and competitive China-Australia services cooperating with Evergreen and APL in order to meet customer demand in Australia and improve customer services.”

Meanwhile, HMM is now in the progress of elevating its Australian agent to subsidiary status to strengthen its position in Australia and provide more stable services. The process will be completed before launching the new service.
