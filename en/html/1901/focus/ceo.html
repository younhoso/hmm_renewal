CEO New Year's Message

"Let's Move
Forward Vigorously
Towards the Great
Future of HMM!"

Dear Colleagues!

New Year's Day of the year 2019 has arrived.

Happy New Year to all of our employees!
I wish you all the fulfillment of your New Year's resolutions and wishes.

In addition, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to all of you who have done your best in your various positions during the past year.

I want to especially recognize offshore members of HMM who are devoting their efforts to improve navigation safety and reduce fuel costs. Please know your efforts are deeply appreciated by us all.

Last year, we steadily made preparations for a new leap in the midst of difficult internal and external circumstances, including US sanctions on Iran, soaring oil prices, and global trade conflict, achieving a 150% increase in handling cargo volume from 3 million TEUs in 2016 to 4.5 million TEUs in 2018, thereby restoring the trust of our customers.

We have launched the Asia Europe Express (AEX) service, which links Asia-Northern Europe, as a basis to prepare for the introduction of mega vessels in 2020. We have also introduced M/V HMM Promise and HMM Blessing, the first mega containerships in the world to be equipped with scrubbers, so that we are able to preemptively cope with changes in the competitive environment caused by new environmental regulations.

In addition, we have achieved a sustainable profit-generating structure through the long-awaited signing of twenty eco-friendly mega container ships, and we are now able to maximize profitability by reducing terminal handling charges and stable operation by re-securing operation rights of the Hyundai Pusan New Port terminal.

Dear HMM Colleagues!

As I have stressed to you many times over the years, 2019 will be even more important year for HMM.

The future of HMM after 2020 will be determined by our achievements during 2019.

In order to operate twelve container ships of 23,000 TEU class from the second quarter of 2020 without interruption, all departments including sales, management, operation and IT need cooperate and prepare carefully together.

In addition, five VLCC vessels, which will be deployed every two months starting from January of the new year, should improve mid-and long-term profitability and growth based on long-term contracts that generate stable profits. This structure will also enable flexible response to market conditions, including the spot market.

To this end, we will have to reform our global organization in preparation for operating upsized vessels through reorganization as well as the complementation and relocation of human resources.

Additionally, we need to promote the ongoing innovation of our business processes - such as building of the cloud-based next generation IT environment - as planned.

In order to facilitate enterprise profit improvement activities, including improvement of customer services and reduction of micro management, we need to enhance our education system which will improve the capabilities of our human resources and the organization.

In the meantime, many countries around the world have already started to apply regulations that meet new international standards before the 2020 environmental regulations deadline.

We need to gather wisdom so that we can turn the crisis of the environmental regulation paradigm change into an opportunity with a preemptive and quicker response than our competitors.

All shipping and maritime institutions predict that the uncertainty of the shipping industry will become even higher this year.

Since the ability to respond quickly is a key-factor that determines success or failure under such an unpredictable environment, please share information collected from internal organizations around the world in real-time between headquarters and local offices as well as departments at headquarters, and based on this, build a system whereby headquarters can make quick and accurate decisions.

Dear HMM Colleagues!

“MOSO Bamboo,” which is rare bamboo that can only be found in the Far East of China, grows only 3cm after four years of sowing, but from the fifth year onwards, it grows to over 30cm in a day and forms a dense bamboo forest in just six weeks.

Developing dramatically in such a short period is called a "Quantum Leap." Just as MOSO bamboo can powerfully stretch out when the time comes, we will make preparations firmly rooted in the ground during a four-year period of difficulty.

The seeds of HMM's Quantum Leap were sown two years ago and 2020 will be a year of leap for us.

Let's steadily prepare for the leap this year and put an end to this difficult period.
I am sure that the fruits of our efforts will result in a great reward.

This leap will be led by newly ordered mega vessels that are competitive in terms of hardware. However, this should be accompanied by software, including a global network, IT and improvement in customer services, based on our sales strength.

The time for our Quantum Leap is approaching.
The pain of growth is retreating.
Let us all become one and move forward vigorously.

Let's communicate and support each other and make a strong move forward for the great future of HMM.

Finally, I wish you and your family members abundant health and happiness together in the new year.

Thank you.

2019. 01. 02

C. K. Yoo
President & CEO
HMM Seoul
